Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Supporter Bot ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your data. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices in connection with our Discord bot service and web dashboard.

By using Supporter Bot, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following categories of data to provide and improve our services:

1.1 Automatically Collected Data

  • Discord User Information: User ID, username, discriminator, avatar URL, and account creation date as provided by Discord's API.
  • Server (Guild) Information: Server ID, server name, member count, channel IDs, role IDs, and server configuration settings.
  • Message Metadata: Message timestamps, channel IDs, and message counts (we do NOT store message content except in ticket transcripts with explicit user consent).
  • Voice Activity Data: Voice channel join/leave timestamps, session duration, and channel IDs for XP calculation and Join-to-Create functionality.

1.2 User-Configured Data

  • Leveling System Data: XP points, levels, ranks, role reward configurations, and leaderboard positions.
  • YouTube Notification Settings: YouTube channel IDs, notification channel IDs, custom notification messages, and last video check timestamps.
  • Time Channel Configuration: Timezone preferences (IANA format), time format settings, and associated voice channel IDs.
  • Reminder Data: Scheduled reminder times, reminder messages, target channels, and recurrence settings.
  • Channel Restriction Settings: Restriction types (media-only, text-only, no-links), bypass role IDs, and immune role configurations.
  • Ticket System Data: Ticket transcripts (full message history), attachment URLs, ticket metadata (opener ID, closer ID, timestamps), admin role configurations, and category settings.
  • Voice Channel (JTC) Configuration: Trigger channel IDs, category IDs, deletion delay settings, user cooldown preferences, temporary channel creation logs, and session analytics.
  • Analytics Data: Server growth metrics, message activity statistics, member engagement scores, weekly snapshots, trend calculations, and historical comparison data.

1.3 Dashboard Access Data

  • OAuth Authentication: Discord OAuth tokens (encrypted), access tokens (short-lived), refresh tokens (encrypted), and session identifiers.
  • Dashboard Activity: Login timestamps, IP addresses (hashed for security), browser user agents, and configuration change logs.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data exclusively for the following purposes:

2.1 Service Functionality

  • To calculate and display XP, levels, and leaderboards across servers.
  • To send real-time notifications when tracked YouTube channels upload new content.
  • To update time channels with accurate timezone-specific information every 10 minutes.
  • To deliver scheduled reminders and recurring announcements.
  • To enforce channel content restrictions and automatically moderate non-compliant messages.
  • To create, manage, and archive support ticket channels and transcripts.
  • To automatically create and delete temporary voice channels in the Join-to-Create system.
  • To generate server analytics reports, health scores, and engagement metrics.

2.2 Service Improvement

  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize bot performance.
  • To identify and fix bugs or technical issues.
  • To develop new features based on aggregate usage data.
  • To prevent abuse, spam, and violations of our Terms of Service.

2.3 Legal Compliance

  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and protect our rights.
  • To respond to valid legal requests from authorities.

3. Data Storage & Security

3.1 Storage Infrastructure

All data is stored in secure PostgreSQL databases hosted on industry-standard cloud infrastructure with the following protections:

  • Encryption at rest using AES-256 encryption.
  • Encryption in transit using TLS 1.3.
  • Regular automated backups with 30-day retention.
  • Access controls limited to authorized personnel only.
  • Multi-factor authentication for database access.
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing.

3.2 Data Retention

  • Leveling Data: Retained indefinitely unless deletion is requested.
  • Analytics Snapshots: Retained for 90 days for historical comparison.
  • Ticket Transcripts: Retained for 1 year or until deletion is requested.
  • Voice Channel Logs: Retained for 30 days for analytics purposes.
  • Configuration Data: Retained while the bot is in your server, plus 30 days after removal.
  • OAuth Tokens: Automatically expire and are deleted after 7 days of inactivity.

3.3 Data Breach Protocol

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours via Discord and email (if provided), and take immediate action to secure the system and prevent further unauthorized access.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

4.1 No Sale of Data

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Discord API: Required for bot functionality. Subject to Discord's Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube Data API v3: Used for video notifications. Subject to YouTube Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Database hosting provider. Subject to Supabase Privacy Policy.

4.3 Legal Disclosures

We may disclose your data if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights under GDPR (EU) or CCPA (California):

5.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. Contact us via our Support Server with your Discord User ID.

5.2 Right to Rectification

You can request correction of inaccurate data through the dashboard or by contacting support.

5.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your data by:

  • Removing the bot from your server (configuration data deleted after 30 days).
  • Contacting support with your User ID for immediate deletion of all associated data.
  • Using the /data-deletion-request command (if implemented).

5.4 Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a machine-readable format (JSON/CSV) for transfer to another service.

5.5 Right to Object

You can object to certain data processing activities. Note that this may limit bot functionality.

5.6 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent at any time by removing the bot from your server or deleting your account.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately for deletion.

Discord's Terms of Service require users to be at least 13 years old (or older depending on jurisdiction).

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission.
  • Compliance with Privacy Shield principles (where applicable).
  • Encryption during transfer and at rest.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our web dashboard uses the following cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Session management, authentication (OAuth), and security (CSRF protection).
  • Functional Cookies: User preferences, theme settings, and language selection.
  • Analytics Cookies: We do NOT use third-party analytics (e.g., Google Analytics). All analytics are first-party and anonymized.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect dashboard functionality.

For significant changes, we will notify users via Discord announcement in our Support Server.
