Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, SupporterBOT collects and stores the following limited data:

  • User ID & Username: To identify you across servers and for the leveling system.
  • Server ID & Configuration: Custom settings, prefixes, and channel configurations.
  • Leveling Data: XP, rank, and level progression within servers.
  • YouTube Configuration: Channel IDs you wish to track for notifications.
  • Analytics Data: Server statistics, engagement metrics, and growth trends for generating health reports.
  • Timezone Preferences: Timezone settings for accurate time display in clock channels and scheduled reports.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the functionality of the bot:

  • To display global and server-specific leaderboards.
  • To send notifications when a tracked YouTube channel uploads new content.
  • To enforce channel restrictions and reminders as configured by server admins.
  • To generate server health reports and analytics dashboards showing engagement trends.
  • To display accurate time in timezone-specific clock channels and schedule weekly reports.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely in our databases. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Access to the database is strictly limited to the bot's developers for maintenance purposes.

Data Retention: Analytics snapshots are stored for historical comparison and trend analysis. Weekly reports are retained for 90 days. You can request deletion of your server's analytics data at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

SupporterBOT uses the YouTube Data API to fetch video information. By using the YouTube notification feature, you also agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your data. You can do this by:

  • Removing the bot from your server (some configuration data may persist for a short period).
  • Contacting us directly via our Support Server to request a full data wipe associated with your User ID.
