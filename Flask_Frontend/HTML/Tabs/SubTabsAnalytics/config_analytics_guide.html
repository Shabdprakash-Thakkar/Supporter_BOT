Understanding how Supporter BOT evaluates your community

How It Works

Supporter BOT Analytics processes real-time events from your server to generate actionable insights. Data is collected continuously and aggregated into weekly snapshots based on your server's configured timezone.

Data & Privacy

  • Only message counts and timestamps are stored.
  • Message content is never saved.
  • Individual user stats are public on the leaderboard.
  • Snapshots are retained for historical comparison.
